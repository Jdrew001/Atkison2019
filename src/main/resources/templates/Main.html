OUR STORY


How We Met

We met at a birthday party several years ago and reconnected while attending the University of Texas at Arlington. While working together to run a campus ministry, we grew close. Our first date was disguised as a "meeting" but we both knew it was much more.

The proposal

Drew and I had planned a short vacation to Paris to spend time with family and visit friends. He had convinced me that he was not going to propose until 2019. My gullible self had assumed this trip was just a family vacation. We spent a few summer days exploring famous sites, making new friends, and eating delicious foods. On our third day in Paris, Drew took me on a detour when he "discovered" a nearby local park. The sun was setting when we stumbled upon a classical gazebo that overlooked glistening lake. We carved our initials into a pillar, confessed our love, and then proceeded to a nearby bench where he proposed.
